How VisitPedia Works

The working of Visitpedia has been demonstrated below:

1.Select a dataset from the home page

2.The first visualisation that you see will be heatmap. Select the year , month and day and click Select, you will be taken to next visualisation The calender view in the center of the visualisation demonstrates the total no. of visit counts for that day of the month and has been color encoded based on the no.of visit counts

3.For the selected timeframe, you should now see all the critical keywords having significant visit count clustered into events and being displayed on the critical event river. If you toggle and go to the text line chart visualization, you shall see the pattern in which the top ten words of the event have been trending depicting whether it was a sudden or continuous event.

4. The last visualisation is a word cloud that clusters the keywords in the event with keywords in the the other events and together depicts a real world event.
